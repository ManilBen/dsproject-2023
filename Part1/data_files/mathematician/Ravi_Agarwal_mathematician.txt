Ravi P. Agarwal (born July 10, 1947) is an Indian mathematician, Ph.D. sciences, professor, Professor & Chairman, Department of Mathematics Texas A&M University-Kingsville, Kingsville, U.S.A. Agarwal is the author of over 1000 scientific papers as well as 30 monographs. He was previously a professor in the Department of Mathematical Sciences at Florida Institute of Technology.
