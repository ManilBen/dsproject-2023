Akimitsu (written: 右光, 彬光, 昭光 or 顕光) is a masculine Japanese given name. Notable people with the name include:

Fujiwara no Akimitsu (藤原 顕光, 944–1021), Japanese noble
Ishikawa Akimitsu (石川 昭光, 1550–1622), Japanese samurai
Akimitsu Itoh (伊東 昭光, born 1963), Japanese baseball pitcher
Akimitsu Takagi (高木 彬光, 1920–1995), Japanese writer
Akimitsu Takase (高瀬 右光, born 1970), Japanese voice actor
